SAINTS/PATROITS MODEL HIGH SCHOOL

Saints/Patroits Model High Schools popularly known as S/P is an educational institution located at Pipeline
New Rumuokwurushi, off east-west road, Port Harcourt, Nigeria. She is popularly known for her display of patroitic
attributes both in uniform styles and physical painting of the building.

ABOUT THE ORGANISATION

This institution was established on the 11th of September,2006 and has since then stood through various challenges
which serves as a threat to its survival. However, despite the various challenges, her resolution of abstinence from any
form of examinationmalpractice remained firm.This institution was founded by Dr. Mrs Theresa Oluchi Okoro- aseasoned
and renowned educationist who has devoted over twenty years of her life in impacting meaningfully into the lives of
students within and outside her vicinity.
